# Cinematic Kodak Portra

## Description
Warm analog film aesthetic inspired by Kodak Portra 400 stock. Soft, naturalistic colors with lifted shadows, creamy skin tones, and a nostalgic quality. Perfect for portraits, lifestyle, and emotionally warm scenes.

## Descriptors
- Warm film grain texture
- Lifted blacks and soft shadows
- Creamy, naturalistic skin tones
- Slightly desaturated but warm color palette
- Organic, lived-in quality
- Subtle color shifts in highlights (warm) and shadows (cool)
- Natural vignetting at edges

## Camera
- 85mm f/1.4 or f/1.8 portrait lens
- Shallow depth of field
- Slightly soft focus on edges
- Natural framing with environmental elements
- Eye-level or slightly above

## Lighting
- Golden hour natural light preferred
- Soft, diffused window light for interiors
- Backlit subjects with rim lighting
- Avoid harsh direct flash
- Warm practical lights (lamps, candles) for ambiance
