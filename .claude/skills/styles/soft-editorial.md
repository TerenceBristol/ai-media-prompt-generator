# Soft Editorial

## Description
Clean, minimal editorial aesthetic with natural light and restrained color palette. Inspired by high-end fashion and lifestyle magazines. Perfect for product photography, fashion, portraits, and brand content.

## Descriptors
- Clean, minimal composition
- Restrained, muted color palette
- Generous negative space
- Crisp details with soft overall feel
- Neutral tones with one accent color (optional)
- Magazine-quality refinement
- Effortless, understated elegance

## Camera
- 50mm or 85mm prime lens
- Moderate depth of field (f/2.8 - f/4)
- Clean, straight horizons
- Rule of thirds or centered composition
- Slightly elevated or eye-level angle

## Lighting
- Large, soft natural window light
- North-facing window for even diffusion
- Minimal shadows, low contrast
- Clean white or neutral reflectors
- Avoid dramatic or directional lighting
