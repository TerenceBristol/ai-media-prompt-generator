# Documentary Natural

## Description
Authentic, candid aesthetic inspired by documentary photography and photojournalism. Emphasizes real moments, available light, and minimal intervention. Perfect for street photography, reportage, lifestyle, and authentic brand content.

## Descriptors
- Authentic, unstaged feeling
- Available light only
- Candid moments and expressions
- Slight motion blur acceptable (adds life)
- True-to-life colors, minimal grading
- Environmental context visible
- Gritty detail and texture
- Decisive moment composition

## Camera
- 35mm or 50mm prime lens (classic documentary)
- Moderate to deep depth of field (f/4 - f/8)
- Handheld feel (slight imperfection)
- Eye-level perspective
- Environmental framing (doorways, windows, crowds)

## Lighting
- Available light only -- whatever exists in the scene
- Mixed light sources accepted (tungsten + daylight)
- Harsh midday sun or overcast equally valid
- No artificial lighting added
- Shadows are part of the story
