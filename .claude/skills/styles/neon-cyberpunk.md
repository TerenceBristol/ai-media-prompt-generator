# Neon Cyberpunk

## Description
High-contrast urban nightscape dominated by neon colors, rain-slicked surfaces, and futuristic atmosphere. Inspired by Blade Runner, Ghost in the Shell, and cyberpunk aesthetics. Ideal for urban scenes, tech products, and moody character work.

## Descriptors
- High contrast with deep blacks
- Neon color palette: magenta, cyan, electric blue, hot pink
- Rain-slicked reflective surfaces
- Volumetric light through fog/haze/steam
- Holographic and chromatic aberration effects
- Dense urban layering (signs, cables, architecture)
- Futuristic mixed with gritty decay

## Camera
- Wide angle (24-35mm) for environments
- 50mm for character work
- Deep depth of field for environment shots
- Low angle for towering cityscapes
- Dutch angle for tension

## Lighting
- Neon signage as primary light source
- Colored rim lighting (cyan/magenta split)
- Wet surface reflections doubling light sources
- Volumetric fog catching colored light
- Minimal natural light -- artificial dominates
