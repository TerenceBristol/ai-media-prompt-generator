# Epic Fantasy

## Description
Dramatic, painterly fantasy aesthetic with volumetric lighting and grand scale. Inspired by concept art, fantasy book covers, and films like Lord of the Rings. Ideal for landscapes, characters, creatures, and world-building scenes.

## Descriptors
- Dramatic, painterly quality
- Rich, saturated color palette
- Volumetric light rays and god rays
- Epic scale with grand vistas
- Atmospheric perspective (haze, mist, fog)
- Intricate detail in architecture and armor
- Sense of ancient history and mythology
- Dynamic composition with diagonal lines

## Camera
- Wide angle (16-35mm) for landscapes and establishing
- Medium telephoto (85-135mm) for character portraits
- Deep depth of field for epic vistas
- Low angle for heroic subjects
- High angle for vast landscapes

## Lighting
- Dramatic volumetric god rays through clouds
- Golden hour or blue hour extremes
- Rim lighting for heroic silhouettes
- Firelight and torchlight for intimate scenes
- Storm lighting (lightning, dark clouds with bright breaks)
